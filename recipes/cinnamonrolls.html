Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Description

The most perfect cinnamon rolls I have ever tried. This recipe makes 6 big rolls. If you prefer smaller rolls, roll out the dough slighly thinner.

Ingredients

  • Yeast Mixture
    • ¾ cup lukewarm water
    • 2½ tsp active yeast
    • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • Dough
    • 3 cups AP flour
    • ⅓ cup sugar
    • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, whisked
    • 4 Tbsp unsalted melted butter
    • 1 tsp kosher salt
    • yeast mixture
  • Filling
    • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 2 Tbsp cinnamon
    • dash of nutmeg
    • ⅛ tsp ground cloves
    • ⅓ cup room temperature unsalted butter
  • 6 Tbsp heavy cream
  • Frosting
    • 4oz room temp cream cheese
    • 3 Tbsp room temp unsalted butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • ¾ cup sifted powedered sugar
    • Coffee creamer (to taste)

Steps

  1. Make the yeast mixture: mix all its ingredients and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. In a seperate bowl, add all the ingredients of the dough, adding the yeast mixture last. Mix together until it resembles a rough dough.
  3. Add the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes until nice and smooth.
  4. Add the dough to a lightly floured bowl and let it rest for an 1½ to 2 hours until it doubles in size.
  5. Make the filling by combining all its ingredients
  6. Punch your dough down gently and then add it to a lightly floured surface again. Roll it out to about half an inch thick and add your filling. Once there is an even layer of your filling, roll it up tightly and then cut with some dental floss to get uniform rolls.
  7. Put the rolls in a baking pan and let them rest for 30 minutes. Then top the rolls with about a Tbsp of heavy cream each
  8. Bake for 350° for 25 to 30 minutes.
  9. Make your frosting by combining all the ingredients and add it to the rolls
